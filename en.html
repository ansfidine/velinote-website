Your Thoughts, Beautifully.

Velinote is a modern note-taking app with rich text editing, task management, calendar events, and smart reminders — all stored privately on your device.

Download on Google Play
📝

Rich Text Editing

Write with the powerful Quill editor. Format text, add headings, lists, checkboxes, and more. Your notes, your style.

✅

Task Management

Stay on top of your to-dos with categories, priorities, due dates, and star marking. Filter and sort tasks to focus on what matters.

📅

Calendar Integration

Create and manage events right inside the app. Month, 2-week, and week views. Event cards with location support and reminders.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Set reminders for notes and events. Get notified exactly when you need to act — never miss a deadline again.

🎨

16 Beautiful Themes

Choose from 16 stunning Material You color schemes. Light and dark mode support with customizable header backgrounds.

🏷️

Tags & Organization

Tag your notes for quick filtering. Archive old notes. Trash with undo support. Full-text search across all your content.

🔒

Privacy First

All data is stored locally on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. Your notes are yours alone.

🔐

Biometric Lock

Secure sensitive notes with fingerprint or face unlock. Keep private thoughts truly private.

🌍

7 Languages

English, French, Spanish, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, and Swahili — all built-in.
